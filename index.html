About Me

Welcome to my portfolio!

I'm a Bioinformatics Developer with a focus on genomics and transcriptomics. My expertise lies in research-oriented biomedical sciences, where I've honed my research and coding skills. Over the past three years, I've tackled real-world challenges through internships and startup companies, delving deep into the intricacies of bioinformatics.

  • Proficient in Python, R, and Bash, with hands-on experience in exploratory data analysis using tidyverse, and pandas. Skilled in data visualization with ggplot, seaborn, plotly, streamlit, and shiny. Experienced in Machine Learning frameworks like Scikit-learn and Keras.
